Retro Arcades in Houston



Houston is home to some of the most exciting retro arcades in the country. Whether you’re looking for a classic pinball machine or a modern video game, you’ll find it in one of the many arcades in the area. From family-friendly arcades to adult-only spots, there’s something for everyone in Houston. Here are some of the best retro arcades in Houston.

The Houston Arcade Expo is the perfect place to experience the best of vintage gaming. This annual event features dozens of classic arcade and pinball machines, as well as a variety of other vintage games. There are also tournaments and competitions, so you can show off your gaming skills.

The 8th Dimension is a great spot for classic arcade gaming. This all-ages arcade features dozens of classic and modern games, as well as a full-service bar and restaurant. Whether you’re looking for a game of pinball or a round of Street Fighter, you’ll find it here.

The Fridge Arcade is a great spot for gamers of all ages. This family-friendly arcade features a variety of classic and modern arcade games, as well as a full bar and restaurant. With free WiFi and plenty of space to enjoy a night of gaming, The Fridge Arcade is the perfect spot for a night out with friends.

If you’re looking for an adult-only arcade experience, then look no further than the Groove Arcade. This 21+ arcade features dozens of classic and modern games, as well as a full-service bar and restaurant. The Groove Arcade is the perfect spot to enjoy a night of gaming with friends.

Whether you’re a hardcore gamer or just looking for a fun night out, Houston is the perfect place to experience the best of retro gaming. With so many great arcades to choose from, you’re sure to find the perfect spot for a night of gaming. So grab your quarters and get ready for some classic arcade fun!
