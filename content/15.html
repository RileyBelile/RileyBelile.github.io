Eric Clapton and his musical career


Eric Clapton is a British musician and singer-songwriter who is widely considered one of the most influential guitarists of all time. He has been inducted into the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame three times. Clapton has had a long and successful career spanning over five decades, with numerous hits and awards under his belt. Join me as I explore his life and music career, from his early days in the Yardbirds to his most recent solo releases.
